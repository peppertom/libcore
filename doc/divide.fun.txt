@name divide
@title divide
@summary Divide two numbers

@description 

Divide two numbers. The asterisk, `/`, is used as an alias for `divide` e.g. `x / y` is equivalent to `divide(x, y)`.

@param {number} value The value to be divided
@param {number} other The divisor
@return {number} Division result

@example divide(x, y)
@example x / y
