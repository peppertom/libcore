@name subtract
@title subtract
@summary Subtraction of two values

@description 

Returns the subtraction of two values. The minus sign, `-`, is used as an alias for `subtract` e.g. `x - y` is equivalent to `subtract(x, y)`.

@param {any} value The number
@param {any} other The other number
@return {any} Result of subtraction

@example subtract(x, y)
@example x - y
